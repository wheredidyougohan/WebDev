My favorite videogames:

Team Fortress 2 Team Fortress 2 has been a popular game for a very long time, and I think this is because of how much fun it can be. It is a class-based movement shooter with teams and large maps filled with props. There are issues of weapon balance and some gameplay "mechanics" that are nearly unjustifiable, but these problems can be for the most part overlooked or played without entirely. I have gotten a lot of time out of this game for how simple it is to pick up, and I still enjoy it, not to mention that it is free. If the problems of annoying weapons or bad game design such as random crits were the extent of this game's problems, then I would highly recommend it. Unfortunately for Team Fortress 2, its development has stagnated and public servers have been plagued with cheaters and aimbots. I cannot say that this game is worth any time to new players in its current state.
Dead by Daylight This game is hosted by incompetent developers who lack a vision of what they want the game to be. Dead by Daylight is an asymetric pvp game with two roles, killer and survivor. Killer is a character-based role on defense in the match, while survivor is played on a team of four and plays offensively. The killer has to defend generators which the survivor repairs to power the escape. The developers seem to be confused about how their game is played. Even after several years of balance changes and additions, map and character reworks, this game is still marketed as an "immersive horror experience." The audience for this marketing doesn't seem to exist, however, and the illusion is sold after a few minutes of play. When the most interesting way for survivors to play is to run in the killer's face and shine flashlights at him, and the best killer is all about prediction and quick reaction rather than stealth, which happens to be arguably the worst way to play, the game is clearly not what it is marketed as. I do not think this is an issue, but it is the way the developers balance and talk about the game that this becomes confusing.
League of Legends This game is very competitive and can be fun, but it is also very easy to become frusturated. Sometimes you can have a fun playing your favorite characters with friends in this fast-paced accessible moba. However, often times when playing you may be matched with inhuman creatures who speak, type, and look like people, but behave very much like animals. This game is all about feeling frustration over incomprehensible situations but continuing to play and later being surprised by something even more stupid, which can be funny.
