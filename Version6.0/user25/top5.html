Top 5 Crackers of All Time

TOP 5 CRACKERS OF ALL TIME (in my opinion)

  • 1 - Cheez-It
  • 2 - Ritz
  • 3 - Goldfish
  • 4 - Saltine
  • 5 - Graham Cracker

Cheez-It

Cheez-It's are the best cracker that have ever tried for multiple reasons, one of which is its variety of flavors. White chedder, Original, and Cheddar Jack. Each of these flavors are very good and are the reason for Cheez-Its being number 1 on this list.

Ritz

Ritz are the perfect cracker for adding other things such as cheese, meat etc. Ritz are also delicious to eat on their own. Overall Ritz are a good cracker for any situation which is hwo it is second on this list.
Goldfish
Goldfish have a few flavors that make it stand out from the others, these being the Flavor Blasted Cheddar and the Original. The Cheddar flavor is the best flavor of Goldfish which is the main reason that it is on this list.
Saltine
Saltines, while plain, are a very good cracker to snack on a cold day with some soup. I also like Saltine's alone.
Graham Cracker
Graham crackers are mainly used for one thing and one thing only, smores. If not eaten with smores, graham crackers are a very plain cracker but in my opinion, smores make graham crackers good enough to be number 5.
